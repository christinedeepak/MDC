My Family

His calm demeanor and sharp mind make him a great problem-solver, and he’s the one I turn to for advice whenever I need it.

Lastly, Catherine Deepak, my sister is the one who adds so much energy and fun to our family. She’s a bundle of joy, always ready to share a laugh and offer a helping hand. Despite our occasional disagreements, we share an unbreakable bond and have a lot in common. Together, we make a team that’s always ready for any adventure.
